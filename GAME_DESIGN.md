# CHROME RISING — Game Design Document

## 1. CORE MECHANICS

### Perspective & Combat
- **Third-person cover shooter** with full 360° aerial combat
- **Gunplay tiers:**
  - **Primary:** Auto Rifles (sustained), Pulse Rifles (burst), Scout Rifles (precision)
  - **Secondary:** Hand Cannons (high impact), SMGs (close), Shotguns (room clearers)
  - **Heavy:** Rocket Launchers (splash), Swords (melee), LMGs (suppression)
- **Aim-down-sights (ADS)** with weapon-specific zoom; hip-fire as risk/reward

### Abilities
- **Grenades (3 types per subclass):** Impact, Pulse (zone denial), Solar (burn DoT)
- **Class Abilities:**
  - **Titan — Barricade:** Deployable hard cover, rechargeable
  - **Hunter — Dodge:** Evasion dash, reloads weapon or recharges melee
  - **Warlock — Rift:** Healing or Damage Buff placement
- **Super (Ultimate):** 3 subclasses × 3 specs each; channeled or instant burst damage with 5–8 min cooldown

### Movement
- Sprint, Slide, Jump, **Double Jump**, **Wall Run**, **Grapple** hook
- Momentum-based; chaining moves rewards skilled traversal
- Grapple: pull to surfaces, enemies, or teammates (救済 mechanic)

## 2. PROGRESSION SYSTEM

### Light Level (Power Score)
- Range: **1–2000**
- Soft cap at 1800 (powerful/pinnacle drops only); cap 2000
- Gear score averaged across all equipped items
- Season pass grants +20 baseline power on reset

### Seasonal Artifact
- Unlockable mod tree per season (20–30 trait nodes)
- Passive bonuses: shield pierce, ability regen, stun bonuses
- Seasonally themed; resets each quarter

### Skill Trees
- **3 Classes** × **3 Specs** (9 total builds)
  - Each spec modifies Super, Class Ability, Grenade, and passive bonuses
- Full respec available at in-game vendor (no real money)

### Weapon Masterwork
- Level 1–10 via kills/usage
- Stat boosts at Tier 6, 8, 10 (Reload, Handling, Range, Stability)
- Masterwork orb generation on multikills

## 4. LOOT & LOOTING

### Rarity Tiers
- **Exotic (Gold):** 1 per slot (armor + weapon); curated unique perks, weekly lockout
- **Legendary (Purple):** Gear sets + world weapons; random rolls on 5 perks
- **Raid Gear (Red):** Exclusive sets, raid-specific mods, Triumph-linked cosmetics
- **Seasonal Armor:** Themed sets, seasonal mod slot (stackable with raid mods)

### Acquisition
- World drops, vendor reputation, seasonal pass, raid completion, Trials passage
- Focusing (spend currency to narrow RNG drop)

## 5. CYBERPUNK SETTING

### World State — NeoTokyo, 2187
Advanced tech fused with urban decay. Megacorp towers pierce smog; street-level markets run on gray-code and chrome.

### 6 Megacorporations (Factions)
| Corp | Domain | Aesthetic |
|---|---|---|
| **NEXUS DYNAMICS** | Biotech / Medical | White chrome, clinical |
| **OMEGA SYNDICATE** | Arms / Mercenary | Military urban, rust |
| **CHROME SYSTEMS** | AI / Netrunning | Neon-lit, digital glitch |
| **HYDRA CORP** | Energy / Agriculture | Industrial green, bio-hazards |
| **AURORA FINANCE** | Commerce / Data | Glass towers, corporate blue |
| **BLACKMERE** | Blackops / Cyberware | Matte black, aug-heavy |

### 7 Districts
1. **The Spire** — Central megatower hub, social space
2. **Underdeck** — Slums, black markets, Hive activity
3. **Neon Sector** — Club districts, Chromatic Gang territory
4. **Industrial Zone** — Factories, Hydra operations
5. **The Breach** — Contaminated quarantine zone, raid entrance
6. **Netropolis** — Augmented reality hotzone, lore vault
7. **Transit Lines** — Connecting tunnels, Gambit map territory

## 6. STORY HOOKS

### Main Questline: Stop the Collapse 2.0
A hidden megastructure beneath NeoTokyo activates — a second **Collapse Engine**, remnant of the pre-war era. Hive intelligence and 6-corp proxy wars converge on the same goal: control or destroy it. The player is caught between factions, each with their own agenda, as the city begins to fracture dimensionally.

### Faction Story Arcs
- **3 Primary arcs** (aligned with player choice): Liberation, Domination, or Neutrality
- Each arc unlocks unique missions, dialogue, and gear at reputation rank 10, 20, 30
- Seasonal lore chapters advance all arcs incrementally

### NPC Companion System
- 4 companions with personal questlines (rank 1–10 affection)
- One-time dialogue options per mission influence their arc
- Companions provide: passive buffs, tactical calls, narrative commentary
- Fully voiced; cinematic cutscenes at rank milestones
